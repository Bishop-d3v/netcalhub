Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Overview

Welcome to NetCalcHub. We provide free, client-side web utility tools for network engineers and student pros. We are fully committed to keeping your computing metrics private and transparent.

2. Data Collection & Processing

Our calculators (including IPv4, VLSM, and Cisco ACL scripts) operate 100% locally inside your web browser. No network addresses, host requirements, or system topologies entered into our calculations are ever transmitted to or stored on our backend web hosting servers.

3. Google AdSense Cookie Disclosure

This website intends to utilize Google AdSense to display non-intrusive advertisements to support server hosting costs.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses specialized DART cookies to serve contextual ads based on user visits here and across broader internet spaces.
  • Users may completely opt out of personalized DART cookie profiles by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Web Frameworks

We leverage modern analytics platforms and content distribution elements (such as Tailwind CSS frameworks) to track general visitor counts and load performance metrics. These tools evaluate aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics to clean up interface design spaces.

5. Consent

By interacting with our networking calculators, you hereby acknowledge and grant consent to the tracking parameters outlined inside this formal Privacy Policy framework.
